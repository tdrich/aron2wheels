The Pig Trail Loop

At roughly 6 hours of actual ride time, this one is truly a full day trip. Including the famed Pig Trail, this loop winds through the Ozarks between Conway and Eureka Springs and may be easily begun from either end. Those wishing to avoid the freeway may alternately take US route 64 from Conway to Ozark. Near beautiful Beaver Lake, Eureka Spring offers a wealth of recreational opportunities, tourist stops and biker friendly businesses. In truth it almost difficult to fins a road in this area that is not fun to ride.
