Hot Springs To Bull Shoals

AR 7, from Historic Hot Springs to Diamond City on Bull Shoals Lake

This winding route will take you from historic Hot Springs, AR, frequented by both gangsters and Hollywood's elite, up the Arkansas Scenic 7 byway through Russelville and past beautiful lake Dardanelle. The route then continues up through Jasper, AR where you’ll pass the entrance of Dogpatch USA, a defunct theme park based on the Li’l Abner comics of the 1930’s to the 1970’s. The route then passes thorough Harrison, AR on the way to its finish in Diamond City on the shore of Bull Shoals lake. Fuel and food can be found in most of the towns along the route with the greatest variety to be found in Hot Spring and Russelville, although the Ozark Cafe in Jasper makes a pleasant lunch stop, offering well regarded hamburgers and other diner fare. There is lodging available at both ends of the route including lakeside lodging in Diamond city. For skilled riders, check The Mt. Nebo Run as its beginning is just off hwy 7 and offers wonderful views from the top of Mt Nebo
