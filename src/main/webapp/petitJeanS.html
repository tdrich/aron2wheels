The Petit Jean Mountain Short Loop

A jaunt over PetitJean Mountain

Clocking in at a brief 45 min, this quick outing is great for those staying on or near Petit Jean State Park but would prefer a shorter ride than The Petit Jean Long Loop. Winding down one side of Petit Jean Mountain and up the other after passing through some hilly ranch lands and river bottom farm lands, this loop ig a great exposure the kind of roads Arkansas has to offer.
