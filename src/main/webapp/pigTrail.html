The Pig Trail

The Pig Trail Plus

This ride between Ozark and Eureka Springs includes the well known Pig Trail Scenic Byway between Ozark and Brashears Arkansas. However, as the name implies we’ve added a little more to the ride to either begin or end in the lovely town of Eureka Springs which offers a wealth of recreational opportunities, tourist stops and biker friendly businesses. In truth it almost difficult to find a road in this area that is not fun to ride. Food and fuel are available at both ends of the ride and AR on 2 Wheels recommends Workman’s Travel Center on the outskirts of Ozark as an excellent place to stretch your legs and get a drink before topping off your tank and heading on.
