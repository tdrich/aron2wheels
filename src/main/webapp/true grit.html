Follow The True Grit Trail

Follow in Maddie Ross's Footprints

For all you riders with true grit, or fans of either the 1969 John Wayne or 2010 Jeff Bridges version of the film, have we got a trip for you. Follow the footsteps of Maddie Ross from Dardanelle to Fort Smith on her way to find her father’s murderer and see justice done. Since this page is about rides in Arkansas, this ride ends in Fort Smith near the border with Oklahoma. However, adventurous ride can continue the journey into Oklahoma and then south from Sallisaw to the winding stair mountains
