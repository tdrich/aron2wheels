Petit Jean Long Loop

Over Petit Jean Mountain, The Long Way Around

This loop is a slightly longer loop than the The Petit Jean Mountain Short Loop, it is a great ride for anyone staying in or near Petit Jean State Park. This loop offers a blend of open straightaways, tight turns and open sweeping curves. Fuel is available in Danville(just off your route on E 8th st, Perryville, and Oppelo. Food is available at the historic Mather Lodge within Petit Jean state park, Danville, and Perryville
