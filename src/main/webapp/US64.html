US Route 64

Beebe to Fort Smith, and Everything in Between

A pleasant ride through small town Arkansas for those interested in what traveling the state would have been like prior to the creation of interstate 40. This ride follows a section of the 2,326mi US Route 64 through small town as it roughly parallels the interstate from Beebe to Fort Smith. Fuel, small town eats and small town sights abound along this route. It is worth noting however that a Federal Route is not a specific high way but a path. Attention should be paid to signage when passing through larger towns to be sure you stay on the correct route.
