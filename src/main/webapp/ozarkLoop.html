Loop Through the Ozarks

A Ride Through the Ozarks with a Well Known Biker Stop

The Ozark Loop brings riders on a winding loop through the Ozark mountains beginning and ending in Morrilton, AR with Mountain View making a convenient lunch stop in either direction. Another stop along the way, almost a required stop for Arkansas riders in the area, is The Drasco Trading Post, here a rider can find motorcycles and gear for sale, a place to stretch, warm up or cool off, and biker friendly staff and patrons. The loop also takes riders past Greers Ferry Lake which offers stunning views, boat rentals, lodging and for those interested in something different, an active fish hatchery used to stock the lake
