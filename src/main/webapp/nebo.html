The Mt. Nebo Run

AR 155, 2 miles of wild to the top of Mt. Nebo

Referred to as 2 miles of wild, this quick run can be done in less than 15 min one way. Starting in Dardanelle, AR and ending at Sunset Point on the top of Mt. Nebo in Mt, Nebo State Park. HWY 155 is the sole route up or down the mountain and includes 11 hairpin turns with up to an 18% grade totaling over 1000â€™ in elevation change. Please note: This route is recommended for skilled riders only, and there are no food or fuel services within the park. The park has wonderful views of the Arkansas river valley from Sunset Point, Sunrise Point and the visitor center
